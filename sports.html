Sports

Check out these sports:

Football

Football, or soccer, involves two teams of 11 players each trying to score by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal. Key skills include dribbling, passing, and shooting. Basic training includes practice drills for these skills and fitness exercises.

Basketball

Basketball is played with two teams of five players each. The objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop. Essential skills include dribbling, shooting, and defense. Regular practice should focus on ball handling, shooting accuracy, and team play.

Swimming

Swimming is a full-body workout that involves moving through water using your arms and legs. Common strokes include freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly. Training should focus on technique, endurance, and breathing.

Tennis

Tennis involves two players (singles) or two teams of two players each (doubles) using rackets to hit a ball over a net into the opponent's court. Key skills include serving, volleying, and baseline play. Regular practice should emphasize footwork, stroke techniques, and match play strategies.
